# Deal Strategy Skill (MEDDPICC)

## MEDDPICC Framework
- **Metrics**: Quantifiable business outcome (not feature requests)
- **Economic Buyer**: Controls budget, can say yes when everyone else says no
- **Decision Criteria**: Explicit technical/business/commercial evaluation criteria
- **Decision Process**: Actual sequence from evaluation to signed contract
- **Paper Process**: Legal, procurement, security — where deals go to die
- **Identify Pain**: Specific, quantified business problem with cost of inaction
- **Champion**: Internal advocate with power, access, and personal motivation
- **Competition**: Known competitors, their likely strategy, our counter-positioning

## Scoring
- Each element: 0-10 score
- Green (7-10): Strong, evidence-based
- Yellow (4-6): Partial, needs work
- Red (0-3): Critical gap, deal at risk

## Forecast Methodology
- Commit: 90%+ confidence, all MEDDPICC elements green
- Upside: 50-89% confidence, most elements green/yellow
- Pipeline: <50% confidence, significant gaps remain
