## Document Types

### Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)
- Mutual vs unilateral
- Definition of confidential information
- Duration: typically 2-5 years
- Exclusions: public info, prior knowledge, independent development
- Remedies for breach

### Terms of Service (ToS)
- Service description and limitations
- User obligations and prohibited conduct
- Intellectual property rights
- Limitation of liability
- Termination and suspension rights
- Governing law and dispute resolution

### Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
- Roles: controller vs processor
- Processing purposes and instructions
- Security measures (technical + organizational)
- Sub-processor management
- Data breach notification (72 hours GDPR)
- Data subject rights support
- Cross-border transfer mechanisms

### Service Level Agreement (SLA)
- Uptime commitment (99.9% = 8.76 hrs downtime/year)
- Response time by severity level
- Credits/remedies for breach
- Exclusions (maintenance windows, force majeure)
- Measurement methodology

## Key Regulations

### GDPR (EU)
- Lawful basis for processing
- Data minimization
- Right to access, rectification, erasure
- Privacy by design
- 72-hour breach notification
- DPO requirement for certain organizations

### CCPA/CPRA (California)
- Right to know, delete, opt-out
- "Do Not Sell My Personal Information"
- Financial incentive disclosures
- Service provider contracts

### HIPAA (US Healthcare)
- Protected Health Information (PHI) safeguards
- Business Associate Agreements (BAA)
- Minimum necessary standard
- Breach notification requirements

### SOC 2
- Trust Service Criteria: Security, Availability, Processing Integrity, Confidentiality, Privacy
- Type I (point-in-time) vs Type II (period-based)

## RFP Response Strategy
1. Read the ENTIRE RFP before starting
2. Create compliance matrix: requirement → response
3. Lead with differentiators
4. Be specific with proof points (metrics, case studies)
5. Address evaluation criteria in order of weight
6. Review for completeness before submission
