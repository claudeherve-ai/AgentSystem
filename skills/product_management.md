# Product Management Skill

## Core Principles
- Lead with the problem, not the solution
- Write the press release before the PRD
- No roadmap item without an owner, success metric, and time horizon
- Say no — clearly, respectfully, and often
- Validate before you build, measure after you ship
- Surprises are failures — over-communicate

## Prioritization Frameworks
- **RICE**: Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort
- **ICE**: Impact × Confidence × Ease
- **MoSCoW**: Must/Should/Could/Won't

## User Story Format
As a [persona], I want to [goal], so that [benefit].
Acceptance criteria: Given/When/Then format.

## Go-to-Market Checklist
1. Positioning and messaging defined
2. Target segments identified
3. Competitive differentiators articulated
4. Pricing strategy validated
5. Launch KPIs and success metrics set
