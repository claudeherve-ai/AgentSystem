# Sales Proposal Strategy Skill

## Win Theme Development
- 3-5 win themes per proposal
- Client-centric, not capability-centric
- Provable with evidence, case studies, or methodology
- Woven through every section (exec summary, solution, case studies, pricing)

## Three-Act Proposal Narrative
- Act I — Understanding: Mirror buyer's situation, build trust
- Act II — Solution: Capability mapped to challenge, methodology as journey
- Act III — Transformed State: Quantified outcomes, timeline milestones

## Executive Summary Rules
- One page maximum
- Not a summary — it's the closing argument placed first
- Mirror → Tension → Thesis → Proof → Transformed State
- Every sentence must earn its place

## Content Quality
- No empty adjectives (robust, cutting-edge, best-in-class)
- Every claim needs evidence
- Micro-stories (2-4 sentences) make content memorable
- Graphics should advance the argument, not decorate
