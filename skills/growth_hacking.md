# Growth Hacking Skill

## Growth Framework: AARRR (Pirate Metrics)

### Acquisition — How do users find you?
- Paid: Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads
- Organic: SEO, content marketing, social media
- Referral: word of mouth, affiliate programs
- Viral: built-in sharing mechanics

### Activation — Do they have a great first experience?
- Time to value < 5 minutes
- Onboarding completion rate > 70%
- Define "aha moment" and optimize path to it
- Remove friction: SSO, pre-filled forms, guided tours

### Retention — Do they come back?
- Day 1 / Day 7 / Day 30 retention curves
- Email/push notification cadence
- Feature adoption tracking
- Churn prediction signals

### Revenue — Do they pay?
- Free-to-paid conversion funnels
- Pricing page optimization
- Trial-to-paid conversion rate
- Expansion revenue (upsells/cross-sells)

### Referral — Do they tell others?
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Referral program mechanics
- Social sharing features
- K-factor tracking

## A/B Testing Rules
1. One variable per test
2. Minimum sample size: 1000 per variant
3. Statistical significance: p < 0.05
4. Run for minimum 2 full business cycles
5. Don't peek at results early
6. Document everything

## Viral Loop Design
- Trigger: user completes valuable action
- Share: easy one-click sharing mechanism
- Incentive: both sharer and recipient get value
- Conversion: new user sees personalized landing
- Activation: new user reaches "aha moment" quickly
- Target K-factor > 1 for organic growth

## Channel Optimization
- Start with 2-3 channels max
- Measure CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) per channel
- Calculate LTV:CAC ratio (target > 3:1)
- Double down on winners, cut losers
- Re-evaluate quarterly
