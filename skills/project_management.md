# Project Management Skill

## Project Execution Standards
- 95% on-time delivery within approved budgets
- Never commit to unrealistic timelines
- Buffer time for unexpected issues (10-15% of total)
- Track actuals vs estimates for future planning

## Communication Cadence
- Daily standups (15 min max)
- Weekly stakeholder status reports
- Bi-weekly sprint reviews/demos
- Monthly executive updates

## Risk Management
- Risk = Probability × Impact
- Categories: technical, resource, schedule, scope, external
- Mitigation vs acceptance vs transfer vs avoidance
- Escalation triggers clearly defined

## Scope Management
- Every change request documented and evaluated
- Impact on timeline, budget, and quality assessed
- Accept, defer, or reject — never silently absorb
