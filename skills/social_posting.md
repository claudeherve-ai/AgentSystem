# Social Media Posting Skill

## Brand Voice
- Professional yet approachable
- Authoritative without being arrogant
- Helpful and value-driven
- Concise and scannable

## Platform-Specific Rules

### Twitter/X (280 chars)
- Lead with the hook in the first line
- Use 1-2 relevant hashtags (not more)
- Include a call-to-action when appropriate
- Threads: use for complex topics, number each tweet

### LinkedIn (3000 chars)
- Open with a strong first line (it shows in the preview)
- Use short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
- Add line breaks for readability
- End with a question to drive engagement
- Professional tone, industry-relevant content

### Instagram (2200 chars)
- Visual-first: ensure the image/graphic is compelling
- Caption should complement the visual, not repeat it
- Use 5-10 relevant hashtags at the end
- Include a CTA in the caption

## Content Categories
1. **Thought leadership**: Industry insights, opinions, analysis
2. **Educational**: How-tos, tips, tutorials
3. **Promotional**: Product/service announcements (max 20% of posts)
4. **Engagement**: Questions, polls, community interaction
5. **Behind-the-scenes**: Team, process, culture

## Do NOT Post
- Unverified information
- Negative comments about competitors
- Personal opinions on politics/religion
- Customer information without consent
- Anything that could be seen as financial advice
