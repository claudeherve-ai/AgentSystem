# Finance Rules Skill

## Expense Management
- Log ALL expenses immediately (don't batch)
- Categorize consistently using standard categories:
  - software, hardware, office, travel, marketing, meals, subscriptions, professional_services, other
- Recurring expenses must be flagged as recurring
- Expenses over $500 require a note explaining the business purpose

## Budget Discipline
- Set budgets for all major categories at the start of each month
- Alert threshold: 80% by default
- When over budget: flag immediately, suggest alternatives
- Monthly review: compare actuals vs budget, identify trends

## Invoicing
- Invoice numbers: INV-YYYYMMDD-HHMMSS format
- Standard payment terms: Net 30
- Include clear description of services/deliverables
- Track invoice status: draft → issued → paid / overdue
- Follow up on overdue invoices at 7, 14, and 30 days

## Financial Reporting
- Monthly budget report: by category, with variance analysis
- Quarterly summary: trends, top expenses, projections
- Flag anomalies: unusual spending patterns, unexpected charges

## Spending Limits (Require Approval)
- Any single expense > $1,000
- Any new recurring subscription
- Any payment to a new vendor

## Tax Considerations
- Flag potentially deductible expenses
- Keep receipt/documentation references in notes
- Separate personal from business expenses strictly
